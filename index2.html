Fresh Food Finder

The Fresh Food Finder is the easiest way to locate farmers markets wherever you may be. The Fresh Food Finder allows you to easily find farmers markets near your current location, or easily search for farmers markets within a state, city, or zipcode.

All of the information displayed within the Fresh Food Finder is freely available from the US Department of Agriculture through data.gov. This data set was last updated on April 25, 2012. The Fresh Food Finder is not responsible for the quality or accuracy of this data.

Should you encounter any errors or discover inaccurate data, please contact us and let us know so we can fix it!
Fresh Food Finder was created using the following free & open source software:
  • PhoneGap
  • jQuery
  • Mustache.js
  • app-UI
  • Leaflet
  • App Icon Template
